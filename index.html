What Is Assisted Stretching?

Assisted stretching is fundamentally different from a traditional massage.

A certified stretch therapist actively moves your body through guided, professional stretches while you completely relax.

This technique allows for significantly deeper flexibility improvements, faster tension release, and targeted mobility repair that you cannot achieve on your own.
